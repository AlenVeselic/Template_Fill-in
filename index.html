The Hosing

The man hoses. He hoses in the morning, he hoses in the evening, he hoses throughout the entire day. What does he hose? Half the day he waters the grass on his front and backyard. When he reaches the backyard, he waters the garden. After he's done with plant life he attacks his driveway, hosing down the path leading to it and the platform itself of dirt and grime. I must wonder how big his water bill must be... After he's done hosing the world he puts away the hose and hoses his wife. Every Sunday. At 6:30pm right after their favorite crime drama ends. The sounds that man makes. I can feel each of my vertebrae shiver in disgust just thinking about it, hope I don't dislocate something.

The Verdict

I'm going to tackle this by focusing on myself and my own interests. I've lost too much time paying attention to James. Sure, he has caused me grief actively without payback. Vengeance seems meaningless at this point. It would only spark an actual rivalry which I know he wants. He's been watching those sitcoms and wants to cultivate such a relationship, but all he's getting is radio silence.

To reinforce this I've been taking baby steps. I've been taking walks with friends and family members and I've also been doing some research on useful hobbies. Me and my wife have also taken up dance classes on Thursdays. So yeah, things are looking good right now. Good bye to those reading and thank you for reading this ventpage.
